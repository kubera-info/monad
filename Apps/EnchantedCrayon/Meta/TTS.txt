en-US
Color picture. Press green button to start coloring. Press the paw to select a picture. With your mouse drag the picture to change it.

Select a color using a pencil. Now press on the picture with a pencil to start coloring. You can color by dragging a button with the selected color to the picture. To erase the picture press the scissors button with a green background. To print the picture press the print button with a white background. To exit coloring any picture press the cross button with a red background. Turn the sound on to listen to help commands.
